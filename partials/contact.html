Directions

Merge onto I-490 East. Take Rt. 96 South exit #28.

From the east:

From I-90 NY State Trwy, take exit # 45 (Rochester).
Take Rt. 96 exit going North.
Stay in your right hand lane; we are in the Cobblestone Court Plaza behind Pier I and across from East View Mall.
